    In this homework, I implement a Q-learning algorithm to solve the
MountainCar-v0 game.

I tried two experiments, one is
\texttt{how\ learning\ rate\ change\ affect\ the\ results}, and the
other is
\texttt{how\ state\ number\ of\ q-matrix\ affect\ the\ results}.

And I provided three diagrams to illustrate the rusults, which are
\texttt{fail\ times\ over\ training\ process} (fail meant the car didn't
catch the flag), \texttt{rewards\ over\ traing\ process} and
\texttt{step\ number\ in\ each\ episode\ over\ traing\ process}.

    \section{Result and Analysis}\label{result-and-analysis}

the basic environments is learning rate in 0.01, state number is 15,
maximum step number is 1500 and training episode is 5000.
